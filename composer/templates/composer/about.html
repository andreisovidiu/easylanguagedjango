EasyLanguage translator is a web application that allows users to translate text from English to EasyLanguage.

Intra-day trading strategies can be created with just a few clicks.

Insert the entry and exit logic and click on the Generate button then simply copy and paste your EasyLanguage code into your MultiCharts or TradeStation compiler.



INSTRUCTIONS

Choose a trigger: choose a type of entry logic.

Time window: choose the time when you want the strategy to be actively looking for entries.

Stop loss: choose the value of the stop loss in US Dollars.

Take profit: choose the value of the take profit in US Dollars.

Trade liquidation: choose the time when you want to close your trade.

Choose pattern: add a pattern that filters your trades (e.g., strategy is active only if the previous trading day was a Doji candle).

Choose direction: choose the direction of the strategy (long and short, long only, short only).
